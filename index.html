PRAYAS

Programm Associating Youth and Society

  • PRAYAS aims to transform the youth's perception by making them cognizant about the existing social challenges and opportunities.
  • PRAYAS through its initiative aims to inculcate a feeling of social responsiveness among the people to endanger new opportunities for uplifting the underprivilleged section of the society.

KGP Blood Connect

1000+ Potential Donors identified
KGP Blood Connect is a social initiative to help those in direct need of blood as efficently as possible. The princile of this initiative is to create a database of all the willing donors in the vicinity and thsese donors are requested to come and donate blood when required.
